✨ **Challenge #072: Designing for Luxury - The Lumina Estates Slider** ✨

For today's challenge, I wanted to move beyond the basic image carousel and create something that feels premium, cinematic, and alive.

Introducing **Lumina Estates** – a luxury property slider designed to showcase high-end real estate with the elegance it deserves.

**Key Features:**
🎨 **Luxury Aesthetic:** A deep, sophisticated dark mode paired with champagne gold accents.
✨ **Glassmorphism:** Controls that feel lightweight and modern.
⏳ **Progressive Auto-play:** A visual timer that guides the user experience.
📱 **Fully Responsive:** Seamless transitions from desktop to mobile.

Building this was a great exercise in balancing visual weight and interaction design.
